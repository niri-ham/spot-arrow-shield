About us

Security and networking, done right.

Spot Arrow builds and supports customizable security and networking systems for homes and businesses across Orlando, FL.

Who we are

Spot Arrow is a security and networking company serving residential and commercial clients in Orlando, FL. We provide customizable security camera systems, AI-capable video surveillance, access control, structured cabling, and business networking — all designed, installed, and supported by our team.

Every property is different. We start by listening to how you use your space and what you actually want to protect, then design a system that fits — no overselling, no generic packages, no shortcuts in the install.

What we do

From a single doorway camera to a multi-building surveillance and access-control deployment, our work covers the full stack: site assessment, equipment selection, clean cabling, configuration, remote monitoring setup, and ongoing technical support.

  • High-quality AI-capable cameras
  • Video surveillance systems
  • Access control systems
  • Structured cabling
  • Business networking
  • Remote monitoring & support
